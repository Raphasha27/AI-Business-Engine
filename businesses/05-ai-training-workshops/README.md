# Business #5: AI Training & Workshops 🎓🤖

**Zero-Capital: Teach Professionals & Students How to Use AI**

## The Opportunity

Everyone wants to learn AI but doesn't know where to start. Companies need to upskill staff. Universities want AI curriculum. Youth need digital skills. You can teach them — using free tools and free hosting.

## Free Tools Stack

| Tool | Use | Cost |
|------|-----|------|
| **Google Slides** | Presentation decks | Free |
| **Google Meet/Zoom** | Live workshops | Free (up to 100) |
| **Canva AI** | Workshop materials, certificates | Free |
| **Streamlit** | Interactive demos | Free hosting |
| **YouTube** | Course hosting + traffic | Free |
| **Google Classroom** | Course management | Free |
| **Notion** | Curriculum, resources | Free |
| **Calendly** | Booking | Free |

## Workshop Formats

### 1. Corporate Training (R5,000-R15,000 per session)
- "AI for Business Efficiency" (2-hour workshop)
- "ChatGPT for Customer Service Teams" (half-day)
- "AI-Powered Marketing & Content Creation" (full-day)

### 2. Public Workshops (R300-R800 per person)
- "AI for Job Seekers" (3-hour Saturday workshop)
- "Start Your AI Side Hustle" (half-day)
- "Parents' Guide to AI: What Your Kids Need to Know" (90 min)

### 3. University/College Guest Lectures
- "AI in the Real World" (60-90 min)
- "Building Your First AI App" (2-hour hands-on)
- Usually R2,000-R5,000 per session

### 4. Online Courses (R500-R2,000 per enrollment)
- "AI for South Africans: Complete Beginner's Guide" (10 modules)
- "ChatGPT Mastery for Professionals" (8 modules)
- "Build AI Apps with Python (No Experience Needed)" (12 modules)

## Workshop Curriculum Template

```
Module 1: What AI Actually Is (20 min)
  - Demystifying AI with real SA examples
  - Tools overview: ChatGPT, Claude, Canva AI
  - Live demo: Generate a business plan in 30 seconds

Module 2: ChatGPT for Your Work (30 min)
  - Writing emails, proposals, reports
  - Analyzing data, creating presentations
  - Hands-on exercise: Generate a client proposal

Module 3: AI for Content Creation (30 min)
  - Social media content with AI
  - Image generation (Canva AI, DALL-E)
  - Hands-on: Create a marketing post

Module 4: Build Your Own AI Tool (40 min)
  - No-code AI using Streamlit
  - Create a business tool in 20 minutes
  - Hands-on: Deploy to the internet (free)

Module 5: Ethics & The Future (15 min)
  - AI risks and how to avoid them
  - Jobs AI will create vs replace
  - Your AI action plan
```

- [ ] Deliver first workshop flawlessly → collect video/testimonials

## Pro Tips

1. **Record everything** — one workshop = one online course
2. **Partner with co-working spaces** — free venue in exchange for exposure
3. **Target HR managers** — they have training budgets
4. **Create a certification** — people pay more for certificates
5. **Use SA examples** — loadshedding AI, Zulu chatbots, township economy
