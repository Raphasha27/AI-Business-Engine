# Business #1: AI Content Studio ✍️🤖

**Zero-Capital AI-Powered Content Creation Agency**

## The Opportunity

SA businesses need content — social media posts, blog articles, video scripts, email newsletters — but can't afford traditional agencies. Use free AI tools to produce high-quality content at scale for a fraction of the price.

## Free Tools Stack

| Tool | Use | Cost |
|------|-----|------|
| **Claude AI** (claude.ai) | Long-form writing, strategy | Free |
| **ChatGPT** (chatgpt.com) | Social media copy, ideas | Free |
| **Canva AI** (canva.com) | Graphics, presentations | Free tier |
| **CapCut** (capcut.com) | Video editing, captions | Free |
| **Google Drive** | Client management | Free |
| **WhatsApp Business** | Client communication | Free |
| **LinkedIn** | Client acquisition | Free |
| **WordPress.com** | Blog hosting | Free |

## Pricing Model

```
Social Media Package:   R1,500/month  (12 posts + 4 graphics)
Blog Package:           R2,500/month  (4 articles + SEO)
Email Newsletter:       R1,000/month  (4 emails)
Video Scripts:          R800/video    (2-min script)
Complete Package:       R4,000/month  (all of the above)
```

## Pro Tips

1. **Specialize in SA industries** — spaza shops, logistics, township tourism
2. **Use AI for ideation** — Claude generates 50 post ideas in 2 minutes
3. **Reuse and remix** — one blog post = 5 social posts + 1 email + 1 video
4. **Bundle with Canva templates** — clients love ready-to-post graphics
5. **Ask for referrals** — offer 1 month free for every referral
