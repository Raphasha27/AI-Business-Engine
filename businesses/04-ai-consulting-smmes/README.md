# Business #4: AI Consulting for SMMEs 🏪🤖

**Zero-Capital: Help Township & Small Businesses Leverage AI**

## The Opportunity

98% of SA businesses are SMMEs. Most don't know how to use AI, ChatGPT, or even basic digital tools. You can be the expert who shows them — with no upfront cost to you.

## Target Markets

### 1. Township Spaza Shops & Tuck Shops
- Problem: Manual inventory, no digital presence
- Solution: Show them WhatsApp catalog, Airtable inventory
- Value: R1,500 consultation → saves R5,000/month in wastage

### 2. Local Salons & Barbershops
- Problem: No booking system, missed appointments
- Solution: Free Google Calendar booking link + WhatsApp reminders
- Value: R1,500 → adds R3,000/month in recovered appointments

### 3. Delivery & Logistics SMMEs
- Problem: Manual dispatch, no tracking
- Solution: Google Sheets + Make.com automation
- Value: R2,500 → saves 20 hours/week admin time

### 4. Township Restaurants & Catering
- Problem: No online menu, no delivery system
- Solution: WhatsApp menu + Google Forms ordering
- Value: R1,500 → adds R5,000/month in orders

## 30-Day Launch Plan

### Week 1: Build Credibility
- [ ] Audit 3 local businesses for free → build case studies
- [ ] Create a simple website (GitHub Pages — free)
- [ ] Get Google My Business listing
- [ ] Create 5 before/after case studies

### Week 2: Content & Authority
- [ ] Post 2x daily on LinkedIn about AI for SA SMMEs
- [ ] Record 1-min tip videos on TikTok/Instagram
- [ ] Write blog: "5 Free AI Tools Every SA Small Business Needs"
- [ ] Join 10 SA small business groups on Facebook/WhatsApp

### Week 3: Outreach Campaign
- [ ] DM 20 local business owners on Instagram daily
- [ ] Visit 5 local shops in person (wear professional clothes)
- [ ] Offer free 30-min consultation (no obligation)
- [ ] Follow up with all leads within 24h

### Week 4: First Paid Clients
- [ ] Convert 3 free consultations to paid Bronze packages
- [ ] Deliver exceptional value → ask for referral
- [ ] Create a case study with photos/testimonials
- [ ] Upsell to Silver/Gold packages

## Pro Tips

1. **Dress professionally** when visiting township shops
2. **Speak their language** — Zulu/Xhosa is a superpower here
3. **Show, don't tell** — open your laptop and demonstrate
4. **Focus on ROI** — "This will save you R2,000/month"
5. **Offer a guarantee** — "If you don't save money, it's free"
