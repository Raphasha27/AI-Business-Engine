# Business #2: AI Automation Agency ⚙️🤖

**Zero-Capital Business Process Automation for SMMEs**

## The Opportunity

SA small businesses waste hours on repetitive tasks — invoice generation, customer follow-ups, appointment booking, inventory tracking. You can build them free AI automations using no-code tools and charge a monthly retainer.

## Sample Automation (Make.com Flow)

```
Trigger: Google Form submitted (new order)
  ↓
Action 1: Create row in Airtable (order database)
  ↓
Action 2: Send WhatsApp message via Twilio (order confirmation)
  ↓
Action 3: Create invoice in Google Sheets
  ↓
Action 4: Schedule follow-up reminder in 7 days
  ↓
Action 5: Send daily summary to business owner via email
```
